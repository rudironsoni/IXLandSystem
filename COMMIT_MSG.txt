Fix fs/exec.c constant surface - use verified Linux UAPI constants

Tranche 2: Remove local hardcoded fallback constants from fs/exec.c.

Problem:
fs/exec.c had local #defines for O_RDONLY, O_CLOEXEC, FD_CLOEXEC to avoid
struct flock conflict with Darwin headers when including <linux/fcntl.h>.

Solution:
Create include/ixland/fcntl_constants.h with constants-only definitions.
Values verified against Linux UAPI source:
- O_RDONLY: 00000000 (from asm-generic/fcntl.h)
- O_CLOEXEC: 02000000 (from asm-generic/fcntl.h)
- FD_CLOEXEC: 1 (from asm-generic/fcntl.h)

Changes:
- New include/ixland/fcntl_constants.h with verified Linux UAPI values
- fs/exec.c: Include new header instead of local defines
- fs/exec.c: Keep backing_io_decls.h (correct fs-bridge seam)
